In order to play, you must select a number of ships from the drop down and then hit start game. After that, the players take turns dragging their ships onto the grid. The rotate button rotates all unplaced ships, and all ships when dropped will have their left/top side placed where the mouse is released. Once setup is finished, each player takes turns choosing a target square on the left grid and firing until one player has destroyed all the other player's ships. The right grid displays your own ships, with your opponent's hits and misses marked there as well.
